# OMR Sheet Setup Guide

## Physical Requirements

### Paper Size
- **A4**: 210mm × 297mm
- **Quality**: 80 GSM minimum
- **Color**: White background

### Layout
- **Top Section**: Student Information (Roll, Name, Class)
- **Middle Section**: OMR bubbles (typically 4 options per question)
- **Bottom Section**: Instructions

## Bubble Specifications

### Size
- **Diameter**: 4-6mm (circular)
- **Oval**: 5mm width × 8mm height
- **Spacing**: 8-10mm between bubbles

### Arrangement
- **Columns**: 4 (A, B, C, D)
- **Rows**: Based on number of questions
- **Grid spacing**: Uniform, 10mm minimum

## Printing Guidelines

1. **Resolution**: 300 DPI minimum
2. **Ink**: Black for lines and bubbles
3. **Contrast**: High contrast preferred
4. **Quality**: Test print before mass production

## Scanning Requirements

### Scanner Settings
- **Color Mode**: Grayscale or Black & White
- **Resolution**: 300-400 DPI
- **File Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Compression**: Minimal compression

### Tips for Best Results
- Ensure proper paper feed
- Keep scanner clean
- Use automatic document feeder if available
- Scan at consistent settings

## Student Marking Guidelines

### How to Mark
1. Use **HB or 2B pencil** (not ballpoint pen)
2. **Fill completely** - cover at least 60% of bubble
3. **One mark per question** - mark only the correct option
4. **Dark marks** - use moderate to dark pressure
5. **No stray marks** - avoid marking outside bubbles

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ❌ Marking multiple options
- ❌ Partial filling of bubbles
- ❌ Erasing and re-marking (causes smudges)
- ❌ Using pen (creates thick lines)
- ❌ Leaving stray marks near bubbles

## Quality Checklist

Before scanning:
- ✓ All questions answered or deliberately left blank
- ✓ No stray marks or damage
- ✓ Bubbles filled clearly and completely
- ✓ Roll number and name filled
- ✓ No folds or creases
- ✓ No water damage or stains

## Storage

- **Keep originals** for 30 days after evaluation
- **Store in dry place** away from moisture
- **Organize by batch** for easy retrieval
- **Archive digital scans** separately
